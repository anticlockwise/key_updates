Weekly Production Status Update (Oct 29th)

Learn about ongoing projects here! Updates weekly on Thursdays with the Kono Newsletter.

Ctrl + F to find what you're looking for. Contact support@kono.store with any questions or concerns.

If we've missed something that you need info on, let us know.

Coronavirus is still causing delays, mostly ripple effect from the period of downtime at this point. Many projects (and updates on them) will be at stand still for the next few months. Almost all timelines are subject to change once we get updates from the various manufacturers.

EPBT Ivory

Rama caps have arrived. The keycap sets are being produced now and should complete in the next few months. There's not a firm timeline yet, but should be by the end of the year.

EV-01 Switches

Durock has confirmed they are on schedule, with estimated production completion mid-November.

GMK Copper

Purchase order was placed late February. Production was estimated for 24 weeks and then will have another month of transit from GMK to our distribution center. Estimated shipping was for September/ October, but there are couple of projects delayed by GMK that need to ship first. This should be one of the next sets completing from them, after Yugo.

GMK Deku

Group Buy closed August 7th. GMK's estimated production was 28 weeks from our order placement, which puts it around March/April, with another month or so of transit to us before shipping to customers. Pre-orders for extras are live now.

GMK Evil Dolch

Order was placed with GMK mid-September. Estimated production completion was 28 weeks, which puts it around April, with another month or so of transit to us before shipping to customers. Pre-orders for extras are live now.

GMK Firefly

These just arrived at port! It will take a few weeks before they are shipping out depending on customs and check-in. Customs took longer than expected, estimated arrival second week of November.

GMK Fuyu

Fuyu got to us sooner than expected and is shipping now! Extra stock will go up once open shipments are covered.

GMK Hero

The Group Buy closed and the order was placed in March. Product packaging was requested and provided. Estimated production completion from GMK was originally September. There are delays due to a few GMK sets earlier on the pipeline having not been completed yet.

GMK Ishtar

The order has been placed with GMK. Estimated production completion was 28 weeks, which puts it around March 2021, with another month or so of transit to us before shipping to customers. Pre-orders for extras are live now.

GMK Prepress

The order has been placed with GMK. Estimated production completion was 28 weeks, which puts it around March 2021, with another month or so of transit to us before shipping to customers. Pre-orders for extras are live now.

GMK Posh

Some vendors are being slow to communicate final numbers, but we should be placing the order with GMK soon. It is likely that 40s will need to be cancelled, no actions need to be taken as any affected orders will be edited/ refunded automatically. If you have any concerns please email support@kono.store.

GMK Shoko R2

The Group Buy has closed and the order has been placed. Estimated production from GMK was 28 weeks from order placement with them, with another month or so of transit before it ships to customers. Estimated completion is April/May. Pre-orders for extras are live now.

GMK Spirit

This has completed production at GMK and we have organized freight. Estimated shipping to customers late November/early December.

GMK Ursa

These have just cleared customs, estimated to arrive at our warehouse mid-November and ship out to customers soon after.

GMK Yugo

Order was placed in Februrary with GMK for production. Their production estimate was originally July. This is one of the next sets that should be completing from GMK.

Hexgears Gemini, Venture & X-1

Many of these have started to ship out! Feel free to reach out to support@kono.store if you have an open order and would like it adjusted.

Infinity Ergodox

We are going to send a team member out to China to get this project moving as soon as it is safe and possible.

KAT Cyberspace

Order has been placed and we've been invoiced by the manufacturer. Estimated production completion is end of Q1 2021, with about another month of transit before shipping to customers.

KAT Oasis

Production is underway. Estimated completion by Keyreative was originally late summer, but was adjusted for later this year after the date passed. Completion should be by the end of the year.

KAT Specimen

Production is underway. Keyreative adjusted their original estimation from late summer to October/November.

Keystone Mechanical Keyboard

We have just posted a new Kickstarter Development Update!

Email support@kono.store with any questions or concerns.

Kira Stealth / Upgrade Cases / Keycaps

We are in regular communication with the manufacturer and are trying to figure out the best and quickest way to move this project to completion.

Kono Deskmats

Batch 2 Deskmats are shipping now! Samples are being produced for batch 3 now, with full batch to be placed after inspection.

SA Granite

We received samples, check them out on the product page! These are fully underway in production and should be completed in the next month or so. Transit will take about another month after that to make its way from China before shipping to customers.

If you need to make any changes to your order, please contact support@kono.store.
